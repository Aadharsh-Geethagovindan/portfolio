## Challenges & Solutions
<Callout title="Challenge">
A masonry layout that looks “dynamic” is hard to keep consistent across many screen sizes. 
</Callout>

<Callout title="Solution">
Used a manual layout strategy that preserves the masonry appearance while remaining predictable across breakpoints.  
</Callout>

<Callout title="Outcome">
Stable layout behavior without unpredictable reflow issues.
</Callout>
