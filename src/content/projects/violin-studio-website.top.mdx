## Overview
A scheduling and content management website for a family-run violin studio with three instructors. Students can create accounts and book lessons online, while instructors manage availability, confirm bookings, and update site content without touching code.

## Key Features
- Student accounts with the ability to schedule lessons with any of three instructors
- Instructor dashboards to manage availability in 30-minute slots, including “set full day” and “clear day” shortcuts
- Booking request workflow: students request a time, instructors confirm, and both sides see confirmed lessons with date/time
- Instructors page with detailed instructor cards (bio, age, and studio details)
- Admin-editable site content directly from the UI (homepage intro, instructor info, etc.)
- Responsive navigation: hamburger menu on mobile, standard navbar on desktop
